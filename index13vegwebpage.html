Types of Vegetarians

1.The Ovo vegetarianism – Allows the consumption of eggs, but excludes meat, chicken, seafood and dairy products from their vegetarian diet chart

2.Ovo-Lacto vegetarianism – Allows excluding meat, seafood and chicken but allows consumption of animal products such as dairy and eggs

3.Ovo-vegetarian: allows eggs but excludes dairy, meat, fish, and poultry

4.Vegan: excludes all animal products, including honey, dairy, and eggs

5.Today’s vegetarians – They exclude all eggs, seafood, chicken, meat and most vegetables (except potatoes). These kinds survive on junk vegetarian foods such as pasta, pizza, burgers, white bread sandwiches, noodles, samosas, vada pav, kachori, pani puri and pav bhaji.
