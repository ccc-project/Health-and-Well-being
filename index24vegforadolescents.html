Veg diet chart for adolescents

Vegitarian foods for weight loss for adolescents:

1.Legumes like beans, lentils, dried peas

2.Diary products

3.Soy products like tofu.

4.Nuts

5.Low-fat diary products

6.Whole grains

7.Fruits

8.Vegetables

calories required:The USDA recommends that 17 year olds consume at least 1600 calories a day and then more if active. Make sure to make half your plate fruits and vegetables for optimal health.
